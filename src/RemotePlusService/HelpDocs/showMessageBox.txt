
Displays a message box to the user.

Format: <button> <icon> caption: <caption> message: <message>

button: Configures which button to use for the nessage.
	Options:
		b_OK
		| b_YES_NO
		| b_OK_CANCEL
		| b_ABORT_RETRY_IGNORE
		| b_RETRY_CANCEL
		| b_YES_NO
		| b_YES_NO_CANCEL
icon: Configures which icon to use.
	Options:
		i_WARNING
		| i_STOP
		| i_ERROR
		| i_HAND
		| i_INFORMATION
		| i_QUESTION
		| i_EXCLAMATION
		| i_ASTERISK
caption: The title of the message box.
message: The body of the message box.
